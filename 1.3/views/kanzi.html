Kanzi the Bonobo's Communication

Kanzi can't actually speak English unlike parrots He has a book of symbols which he points to. He then points to different symbols to convey an idea.

When did he learn this?

Scientists actually wanted to do this to his mother but his mother didn't get the hang of it but Kanzi picked it up really quickly. Then the scientists switched their research over to Kanzi. He kept learning and you can speak to him and he will understand.
